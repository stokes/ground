# Ground Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2024**

## Overview
Ground is a pushup counter app that helps you track your workouts. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
Ground collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Workout history (reps, sets, duration)
- App preferences and settings

## Apple Health Integration
With your permission, Ground can:
- Write workout data to Apple Health
- This data stays on your device and in your iCloud (if you have Health syncing enabled)

## Data Sharing
- We do **not** collect any personal information
- We do **not** send any data to external servers
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking
- All your workout data stays on your device

## Camera Usage
Ground uses the front camera for vision-based rep counting. Camera data is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored or transmitted.
